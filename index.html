About

Building adaptive learning systems

I am an integrated MS/PhD student in Brain and Cognitive Engineering at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Sang Wan Lee. My research asks how learning systems can handle continual change: preserving useful structure, avoiding plasticity loss, and reallocating computation when the environment changes.

Methodologically, I combine reinforcement learning experiments with architectural interventions and theory-driven analysis. I am especially interested in stability-plasticity trade-offs, dynamic computation, and neural mechanisms that can inspire more robust artificial agents.
